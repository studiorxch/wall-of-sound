# Pole

## Summary

Pole, the long-running project of Stefan Betke, is a foundational StudioRich reference for dub-informed minimalism, digital imperfection, and spacious sonic architecture. His work transforms clicks, noise, and restrained bass into immersive environments.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Pole demonstrates how technological artifacts can become the core vocabulary of a musical language. Rather than hiding digital errors, the project embraces them as structural elements, creating environments defined by space, restraint, and gradual transformation.

## Archive Position

Pole is one of the archive's essential references for dub space, signal decay, and minimalist sonic architecture. Alongside Maurizio, Basic Channel, and Jan Jelinek, the project demonstrates how limitation, texture, and spatial processing can become complete worldbuilding systems.

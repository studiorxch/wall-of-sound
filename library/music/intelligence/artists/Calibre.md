# Calibre

## Summary

Calibre (Dominick Martin) is a defining producer in liquid drum & bass, recognized for understated arrangements, rich bass design, and soulful atmosphere. His music demonstrates how emotional depth can emerge through subtle refinement rather than maximal production.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Calibre's catalog is a reference for sustainable groove systems. Tiny shifts in rhythm, harmony, and texture accumulate into immersive environments, making his work valuable for studying restraint, pacing, and long-form emotional development.

# Cabanne

## Summary

Cabanne (Cédric Cabanne) is a foundational StudioRich reference for intricate groove architecture, rhythmic restraint, and the French branch of microhouse. His productions emphasize precision, elasticity, and subtle evolution over overt musical gestures.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Cabanne demonstrates how long-form engagement can emerge through microscopic rhythmic shifts, unusual syncopation, and carefully balanced negative space. His work rewards attentive listening while remaining deeply functional for extended DJ sets.

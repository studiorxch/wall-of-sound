# Oneohtrix Point Never

## Summary

Oneohtrix Point Never (Daniel Lopatin) is one of Warp Records' defining contemporary artists, exploring memory, media, digital culture, and the emotional residue of technology through highly experimental electronic composition.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

OPN is an essential StudioRich reference because his work functions as media archaeology. Rather than building fictional worlds, he reconstructs forgotten digital futures using degraded synth timbres, fragmented samples, broadcast aesthetics, and cinematic structures. His catalog is a blueprint for creating believable technological atmospheres.

### Worldbuilding Value

His albums feel like navigating abandoned operating systems, obsolete media archives, television broadcasts, and speculative futures. The emotional tension between nostalgia and technological alienation is central to his artistic language.

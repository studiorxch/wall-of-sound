# Maurizio

## Summary

Maurizio is the dub techno project of Mark Ernestus and Moritz von Oswald, reducing techno to its essential architectural elements through repetition, delay, resonance, and space.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Maurizio is one of the clearest demonstrations that atmosphere can emerge from subtraction rather than addition. The project transforms rhythm, echo, and texture into environments that feel structural rather than musical, making it a foundational StudioRich reference for sonic infrastructure.

Maurizio is a cornerstone StudioRich reference for sonic architecture. Together with Basic Channel, Chain Reaction, Plastikman, and Jan Jelinek, the project demonstrates how restraint, repetition, and spatial processing can create complete experiential worlds from minimal musical material.

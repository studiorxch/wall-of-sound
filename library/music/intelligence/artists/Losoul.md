# Losoul

## Summary

Losoul (Peter Kremeier) is a foundational StudioRich reference for understated groove architecture, organic minimalism, and hypnotic long-form house music. His work demonstrates how subtle rhythmic movement and careful sound placement can create enduring dance-floor environments.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Losoul shows how deep house and microhouse can achieve longevity through restraint rather than spectacle. His productions rely on microscopic change, swing, and texture, creating systems that reward extended listening and patient DJ programming.

# Sven Väth

## Summary

Sven Väth is a foundational StudioRich reference for cultural infrastructure, community formation, and long-term ecosystem building. Through Cocoon and decades of DJ culture, he demonstrates how music can evolve into a persistent cultural world.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

His greatest contribution is not simply his recordings but his ability to connect clubs, labels, festivals, travel, visual identity, and audiences into a living ecosystem. He shows how communities become infrastructure and infrastructure becomes culture.

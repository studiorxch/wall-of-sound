# Plaid

## Summary
Plaid is the electronic duo of Ed Handley and Andy Turner. Emerging from the early Warp ecosystem through The Black Dog collective, they became one of the defining voices of melodic IDM by combining rhythmic sophistication with warmth and memorable melody.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive
Plaid is a reference for designing systems that feel human despite technical complexity. Their music demonstrates how intricate sequencing, unusual meters, and evolving textures can remain inviting and emotionally resonant.

# Flying Lotus

## Summary

Flying Lotus (Steven Ellison) is a producer, composer, filmmaker, and founder of Brainfeeder. His work merges electronic production, jazz harmony, hip-hop beat science, and cinematic storytelling into richly layered audiovisual worlds.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Flying Lotus is an essential StudioRich reference because he demonstrates how music, visual identity, animation, film, and narrative can function as one interconnected creative system. His catalog is less about genre than about building immersive ecosystems.

## MUSIC Metadata Profile

### Production Mechanisms

### Worldbuilding Value

His projects create futuristic environments rooted in Afrofuturism, science fiction, dreams, spirituality, and urban culture. Albums feel like complete audiovisual experiences rather than isolated collections of tracks.

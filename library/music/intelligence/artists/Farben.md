# Farben

## Summary

Farben is Jan Jelinek's rhythm-focused alias, exploring the intersection of microhouse, click-based minimalism, and subtle groove architecture. Where the Jan Jelinek project often emphasizes abstraction and sonic decay, Farben shifts toward functional movement while preserving microscopic detail.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Farben demonstrates how minimal rhythmic systems can remain engaging through tiny structural changes rather than dramatic arrangement. The project is a valuable StudioRich reference for restraint, groove design, and long-form behavioral composition.

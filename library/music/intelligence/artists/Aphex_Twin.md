# Aphex Twin

## Summary

Aphex Twin is one of StudioRich's foundational references for system-driven composition, digital identity, and sonic experimentation. His catalog functions as an evolving ecosystem of aliases, custom tools, visual mythology, and exploratory sound design.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

His work demonstrates how worlds can emerge from process rather than narrative. Identity fragmentation, machine behavior, procedural variation, signal degradation, and archive-building become reusable creative systems that align directly with StudioRich's interest in infrastructure, observation, memory, and persistent audiovisual environments.

# Barış K

## Summary

Barış K is a DJ, producer, and curator known for blending Balearic, disco, psychedelic, Turkish, and leftfield dance music into immersive long-form journeys. His work emphasizes curation, pacing, and cultural connections over genre boundaries.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Barış K is a valuable StudioRich reference for understanding how eclectic musical sources can be unified through groove, sequencing, and atmosphere. His practice demonstrates worldbuilding through selection as much as composition.

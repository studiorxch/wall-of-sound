# Moomin

## Summary

Moomin (Sebastian Genz) is a foundational StudioRich reference for warm deep house, understated atmosphere, and emotionally resonant groove design. His productions balance sampled textures, restrained percussion, and gentle harmonic movement to create spaces that feel intimate and timeless.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Moomin demonstrates how softness, patience, and subtle development can sustain attention without relying on dramatic musical events. His work is a valuable reference for creating environments through texture, rhythm, and emotional continuity.

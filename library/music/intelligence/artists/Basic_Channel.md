# Basic Channel

## Summary

Basic Channel is one of the foundational references in the StudioRich archive for sonic architecture, reduction, and infrastructure-first worldbuilding. The project of Mark Ernestus and Moritz von Oswald transformed techno into an environmental system where repetition, resonance, and spatial processing become the primary creative language.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Basic Channel demonstrates how complete worlds can emerge from the smallest musical vocabulary. Rather than relying on melody or spectacle, the project builds persistent environments through feedback, repetition, dub processing, and microscopic evolution. It is a blueprint for designing systems that feel alive through behavior instead of narrative.

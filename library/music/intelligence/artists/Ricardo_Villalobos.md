# Ricardo Villalobos

## Summary

Ricardo Villalobos is a foundational reference for long-form minimal techno and microhouse. His work demonstrates how tiny rhythmic changes, extended duration, and restraint can create deep immersion without relying on dramatic structural shifts.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

For StudioRich, Villalobos is valuable as a systems reference rather than simply a DJ or producer. His music illustrates how small iterative changes accumulate into complex environments. This approach maps directly to worldbuilding, interface design, procedural thinking, and slow environmental storytelling.

Creates the feeling of an evolving nocturnal environment where attention is guided through gradual transformation rather than obvious events.

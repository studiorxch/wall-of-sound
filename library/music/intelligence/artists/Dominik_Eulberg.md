# Dominik Eulberg

## Summary

Dominik Eulberg is a foundational StudioRich reference for connecting electronic music with ecological systems, environmental observation, and natural pattern recognition. His work translates landscapes, wildlife, and biodiversity into immersive rhythmic environments.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Eulberg demonstrates that worldbuilding can emerge from careful observation of the natural world. His productions transform ecological relationships, field recording techniques, and organic development into musical systems that feel alive without sacrificing dance-floor functionality.

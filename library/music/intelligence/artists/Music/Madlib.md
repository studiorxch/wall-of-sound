# Madlib

## Summary

Madlib is an American producer, DJ, multi-instrumentalist, and rapper whose body of work demonstrates how sampling, aliases, and archival research can become a coherent creative ecosystem rather than isolated releases.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Madlib is valuable as a reference because his catalog functions as a living archive. Obscure recordings become raw material for new systems, fictional bands become persistent identities, and each alias explores a different creative constraint. The reusable intelligence lies in the workflow, not just the music.

## Key Eras

### Lootpack Era (1990s)

Foundational underground hip-hop rooted in deep sampling.

### Stones Throw Expansion (2000s)

Development of Quasimoto, Beat Konducta, Yesterday's New Quintet, and collaborative projects.

### Mature Archive Era (2010s–Present)

Cross-genre collaborations and continued expansion of a unified archival universe.

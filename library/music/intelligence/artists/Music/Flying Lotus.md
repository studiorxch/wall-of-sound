# Flying Lotus

## Summary

Flying Lotus is a producer, composer, filmmaker, and label founder whose work merges electronic music, jazz, hip-hop, and experimental composition into a cohesive audiovisual ecosystem.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Flying Lotus is a foundational StudioRich reference because his practice extends beyond albums into an interconnected system of music, visual identity, animation, film, technology, and artist development through Brainfeeder. His work demonstrates how an artist can build infrastructure alongside creative output.

## Key Eras

### Early Warp Era (2006–2009)

Abstract beat music rooted in Los Angeles' emerging beat scene.

### Cosmogramma Period (2010–2014)

Expansion into jazz fusion, dense electronic composition, and ambitious audiovisual worldbuilding.

### Brainfeeder Ecosystem (2015–Present)

Continued experimentation across albums, film scoring, directing, and cultivation of new artists.

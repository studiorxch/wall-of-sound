# idealism

## Summary

idealism creates intimate instrumental music centered on restrained piano motifs, warm textures, and understated hip-hop rhythms. The work emphasizes emotional continuity over spectacle.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

idealism demonstrates how minimal musical vocabulary can establish a recognizable world. Small variations in harmony, ambience, and texture create a persistent atmosphere that can inform StudioRich environments, reflective spaces, and memory-driven projects.

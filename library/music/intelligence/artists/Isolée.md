# Isolée

## Summary

Isolée (Rajko Müller) is a foundational StudioRich reference for organic minimalism, microscopic groove design, and playful systems thinking. His music balances precision with warmth, using tiny rhythmic and timbral shifts to create constantly evolving environments.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Isolée demonstrates that complexity can emerge from restraint. His work is valuable for understanding how small-scale variation, unusual textures, and understated humor can transform repetitive structures into living systems without sacrificing functionality.

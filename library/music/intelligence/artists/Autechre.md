# Autechre

## Summary

Autechre (Sean Booth and Rob Brown) is one of the defining acts in electronic music and the Warp Records catalog. Their work treats composition as a living computational system, exploring rhythm, synthesis, probability, and structure beyond conventional song forms.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Autechre is a foundational StudioRich reference because they demonstrate how rules, algorithms, and evolving systems can produce emotionally compelling worlds. Their catalog is less about individual tracks than about designing autonomous sonic environments.

# Sun Ra

## Summary

Sun Ra created a complete artistic mythology in which music, philosophy, costume, poetry, performance, and cosmology formed a unified creative system. His work is a landmark example of worldbuilding through music.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Sun Ra demonstrates that an artist can build an enduring fictional universe through recurring symbols, narratives, visual language, and sound. His work extends beyond composition into culture, identity, performance, and speculative futures.

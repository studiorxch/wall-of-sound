# Nightmares on Wax

## Summary

Nightmares on Wax, led by George Evelyn, is one of Warp Records' longest-running artists. His catalog blends hip-hop, dub, soul, funk, and electronic production into relaxed, groove-oriented environments built around warmth rather than technical complexity.

## Why This Belongs in the Archive

Nightmares on Wax is a reference for atmosphere through restraint. Instead of dense programming, the music relies on deep bass, sampled instrumentation, spacious mixing, and organic grooves to create environments that feel lived-in and timeless.

## Archive Position

Nightmares on Wax represents the soulful, groove-driven branch of Warp Records. Alongside LFO and Plaid, the project helped broaden Warp beyond bleep techno into rich, cinematic downtempo, making it a foundational reference for warm electronic worldbuilding.

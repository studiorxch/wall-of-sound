
### 3.1 The Philosophy of Non-Empty Silence

WOS explicitly rejects true digital silence ($-\infty \text{ dB}$). In a persistent broadcasting infrastructure, "silence" is defined as **the reduction of event density to expose the world’s baseline physical infrastructure.** When urban corridors or transit lines enter the _Dormant State Profile_, the `Audio Layer` does not close its audio gates. Instead, it transitions from structured rhythmic arrangement to wide, atmospheric, low-frequency continuity. The silence must have weight, scale, and geographic truth.

Plaintext

```
[ ACTIVE STATE ]   ────────────────▶  [ SUSPENDED / DORMANT STATE ]
High Event Density                   Low Event Density (The Substrate)
• Subway Rail Clatter                 • Deep Harbor Low-End Drone
• High-Frequency Traffic Pulse        • Distant, Attenuated Horn Drift
• Active MIDI Grid Arpeggios          • Sparse, Filtered Radio Chatter
• Dense Urban Overlay Audio           • Persistent Weather (Overcast Haze)
```

### 3.2 The Substrate Audio Matrix

When primary runtime systems suspend, the `Audio Layer` must continuously generate an atmospheric baseline composed of four core infrastructural elements:

|**Component**|**Acoustic Profile**|**Geographic Anchor**|**Emotional Intent**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Harbor Drones**|Continuous sub-bass hum ($30\text{Hz} - 60\text{Hz}$)|Coastlines, Waterfronts, `HarborZones`|Grounding, scale, industrial vastness.|
|**Distant Horns**|Heavily attenuated, high-reverb, sporadic signals|Navigating `Vessels`, remote shunting yards|Loneliness, deep spatial depth, drift.|
|**Environmental Hum**|Low-mid static, transformer buzz, power grids|`District` density boundaries|Persistent electrical life, cold automation.|
|**Sparse Radio Chatter**|Bandpass-filtered ($300\text{Hz} - 3\text{kHz}$), irregular bursts|Air corridors, `TransitLines`, emergency nodes|Surveillance interpretation, cold human presence.|

### 3.3 The Occlusion & Attenuation Rules

To maintain the absolute rule that **2D owns truth**, the spatial distance between the unobserved dormant system and the active cinematic camera lens dictates the audio profile:

#### 3.3.1 Low-Pass Gating (The Fog Filter)

As a corridor drifts out of broadcast focus, the high frequencies of its active audio signals are systematically decoupled. Tires on asphalt or track noise dissolve first. What remains is a low-frequency rumble that bleeds into the global background atmosphere.

#### 3.3.2 Environmental Silence via Weather

Weather primitives (such as the current _Overcast_ condition or _Harbor Fog_) act as an acoustic dampener. High atmospheric haze or fog must programmatically increase the reverb decay time while lowering the overall volume threshold of active events, pulling the entire world down into the substrate drone.

---

### 3.4 UI/UX Harmony: The "Still-Alive" Indicator

From a viewer perspective, this approach ensures that during long periods of passive inhabitation (e.g., watching a lonely bridge at 4:00 AM), the audio feed functions as an emotional anchor. The steady, low-end rumble of a harbor drone combined with a periodic, distant horn confirms to the viewer's subconscious that **the infrastructure is still breathing**, even if nothing is currently moving through the frame.

> 📜 **Doctrine Rule:** Silence in WOS is never empty space. It is the raw, unmasked acoustic footprint of the planet's infrastructure. When the actors sleep, the geography sings.

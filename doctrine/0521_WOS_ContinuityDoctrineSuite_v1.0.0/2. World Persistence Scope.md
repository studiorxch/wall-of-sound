## 2. World Persistence Scope

### 2.1 Core Philosophical Mandate

WOS rejects the traditional game engine optimization trick of destroying entities the moment they leave the visual frustum. Because the world is a _persistent 24/7 broadcasting infrastructure_, geography and time are absolute truths.

When a primitive (e.g., a vehicle, vessel, or transit unit) exits an active broadcast corridor or moves off-screen, it does not cease to exist, nor does that corridor freeze in time. Instead, the system shifts from **Explicit Simulation** to **Implied Continuity**.

---

### 2.2 The Persistence Matrix

To manage performance without sacrificing atmospheric weight, WOS divides world entities into three persistence scopes based on visibility and telemetry data availability:

Plaintext

```
               [ SIMULATION PROPAGATION ]
                           │
      ┌────────────────────┼────────────────────┐
      ▼                    ▼                    ▼
┌───────────┐        ┌───────────┐        ┌───────────┐
│ EXPLICIT  │        │ IMPLICED  │        │ STOCHASTIC│
│ TELEMETRY │        │ DET DETERMINISTIC  │  ECOLOGY  │
└─────┬─────┘        └─────┬─────┘        └─────┬─────┘
      │                    │                    │
• Real-time data     • Dead-reckoned      • Statistical
• Grounded truth     • Route-locked       • Generative
• High overhead      • Zero visual cost   • Atmospheric
```

#### 2.2.1 Explicit Telemetry Scope (Active Focus)

- **Behavior:** The primitive is actively fed by live telemetry streams (e.g., live AIS marine data, transit schedules) or high-fidelity internal runtimes.
    
- **State:** Fully simulated in 2D truth; fully interpreted in 2.5D presentation.
    

#### 2.2.2 Implied Deterministic Scope (Off-Screen Continuity)

- **Behavior:** When a primitive leaves the broadcast frame or its live telemetry stream capsizes, it is handed over to a dead-reckoning routing engine (`wall/persistence`). It continues to progress along its defined `Route` or track at a mathematically deterministic speed.
    
- **State:** It is executionally masked (using the _Dormant State Profile_), meaning it costs zero rendering power and minimal processing power, but its spatial coordinates are updated. If the camera cuts to a different corridor downstream, the vehicle will appear exactly when and where it mathematically should.
    

#### 2.2.3 Stochastic Ecology Scope (Ambient Background)

- **Behavior:** For ambient elements not tied to rigid real-world data (like general highway traffic, background bird swarms, or weather drifts), WOS utilizes statistical recurrence.
    
- **State:** They are spawned and culled based on a district’s _Density Profile_. However, the _interpretation layer_ must disguise their departure using atmospheric cues (fog, fading light trails, structural occlusion) to imply they have simply traveled deeper into the geography.
    

---

### 2.3 System-Wide Architectural Impact

Choosing **Implied Continuity** over **Temporal Isolation** fundamentally changes how your core pillars operate:

#### 1. Atmosphere: The Weight of the Unseen

- **Impact:** True ambient dread and atmospheric depth come from the weight of a world moving in the dark. If a car vanishes and the system forgets it, the viewer subliminally feels the emptiness of an empty box. If the system preserves its journey downstream, the world retains its "living infrastructure" gravity.
    

#### 2. Realism: Grounded Pacing

- **Impact:** This preserves your rule: _2D owns truth_. A ship moving through a harbor at 12 knots will take exactly 40 minutes to cross an unobserved bay. If the camera cuts back 20 minutes later, the ship is perfectly positioned at the midpoint. Time remains a synchronized constant.
    

#### 3. Future Scheduling & Transitions

- **Impact:** This makes **Multi-Channel Broadcast Orchestration** possible. If Surface A (`Deep Night Drives`) and Surface B (`Harbor Watch`) share intersecting geography, a vehicle can seamlessly transition from one broadcast channel to another because it exists independently of both.
    

---

> 📜 **Doctrine Rule:** The frame of the broadcast is a lens, not a creator. Space and time move forward universally across all corridors. Primitives exiting the frame are not unmounted; they are merely blanketed by the environment, continuing their traversal invisibly until recalled by geography or schedule.

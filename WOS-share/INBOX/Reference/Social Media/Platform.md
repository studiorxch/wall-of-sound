# Platform Publishing Fields

|Platform|Required Fields|Optional Fields|Link Support|Thumbnail/Cover|Scheduling Notes|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|Twitch|Title, Category, Description|Tags|Limited|Channel/video preview|Best for live/longform ambient sessions|
|YouTube|Title, Description, Thumbnail|Chapters, Links, Tags, Playlist|Strong|Custom thumbnail|Best for evergreen discovery|
|Instagram|Caption, Media|Collaborators, Location, Alt Text|Weak|Reel cover|Best for visual excerpts|
|TikTok|Caption, Video|Hashtags, Cover Text|Weak|Cover frame|Best for short clips|
|Pinterest|Title, Description, Link|Board, Alt text|Strong|Pin image/video|Best for visual archive traffic|

# Hashtags

|Platform|Hashtag Minimum|Recommended Use|
|---|--:|---|
|Twitch|3–5 tags|Functional discovery tags: Ambient, Lofi, Music, Chill, Study|
|YouTube|3 minimum|Put 3–5 focused hashtags at bottom of description|
|Instagram|8–15|Mix niche, mood, format, and brand tags|
|TikTok|4–8|Use short discovery tags + format tags|
|Pinterest|3–6|Treat like search keywords, not social tags|
|Threads / X|1–3|Keep light; don’t overload|
|Tumblr|5–12|Best platform for aesthetic/tag layering|
|Facebook|1–3|Minimal, mostly campaign or brand tags|
|LinkedIn|3–5|Only professional/contextual tags|

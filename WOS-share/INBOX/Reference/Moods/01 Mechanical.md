## What they mean

|Tag|Meaning|
|---|---|
|`opener`|Starts a playlist cleanly; establishes tone without demanding too much.|
|`closer`|Feels final, resolving, or suitable near the end.|
|`bridge`|Connects two different sounds, tempos, keys, or moods.|
|`reset`|Clears energy or resets attention after intensity.|
|`lift`|Raises energy, brightness, or momentum.|
|`drop`|Lowers energy or pulls the playlist down.|
|`hold`|Maintains the current state without major change.|
|`drift`|Creates floating, loose, ambient movement.|
|`pulse`|Has steady rhythmic drive.|
|`tension`|Adds unease, pressure, suspense, or unresolved motion.|
|`release`|Resolves tension or opens space.|
|`build`|Gradually increases intensity or complexity.|
|`plateau`|Sustains a high or stable level.|
|`recovery`|Lets the listener breathe after a dense/intense track.|
|`transition`|Useful between sections, not necessarily a major feature track.|
|`anchor`|Strong identity track; defines the playlist’s center.|
|`disruptor`|Intentionally breaks pattern or changes direction.|
|`deepener`|Makes the playlist darker, deeper, more immersive.|
|`brightener`|Adds light, clarity, or melodic openness.|
|`shadow`|Adds darker color without necessarily raising intensity.|

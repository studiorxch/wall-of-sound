# 🎯 PURPOSE

ObservabilityCamera governs continuity-aware spatial framing for WOS broadcast systems.

This system defines:

- camera continuity behavior
    
- atmospheric pacing
    
- symbolic spatial framing
    
- observability weighting interpretation
    
- macro-scale continuity choreography
    
- cinematic continuity translation
    

ObservabilityCamera is an interpretation-layer system.

- own runtime truth
    
- mutate world simulation

- create telemetry truth
    
- control renderer shaders
    
- orchestrate simulation state
    

ObservabilityCamera interprets:

```text
resolved continuity conditions
```

```text
simulation authority
```

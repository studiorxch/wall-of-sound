# Implementation Guide

## 1. Stabilize World Runtime

Fix navigation, camera, atmosphere, and surface separation before introducing new complexity.

## 2. Prototype Landmark Language Externally

## 3. Layer Behaviors Gradually

Introduce audio-reactive materials, transit flow, ecology, and embedded gameplay only after the world feels coherent and alive.

#

## Problem

0605K.1 successfully exposed the camera shot selector UI.

However runtime validation reveals that none of the new camera shots appear to be functioning as true POV views.

Observed behavior:

- Windshield appears nearly identical to Follow
    
- Left Window appears nearly identical to Follow
    
- Right Window appears nearly identical to Follow
    
- Rear Window appears nearly identical to Follow
    
- Bus Front appears as a high aerial map view
    
- Street Level appears as a high aerial map view
    

The camera remains predominantly top-down across all shot selections.

This suggests one of the following:

1. Shot definitions exist but are never reaching the viewport authority.
    
2. Shot definitions reach the viewport authority but are ignored.
    
3. Shot transforms are applied but use placeholder values.
    
4. Camera preset logic is overriding shot logic after selection.
    
5. Terrain-aware enhancement is executing but the underlying shot camera never changes.
